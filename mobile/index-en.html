Vinee
Authenticate Wine Everywhere
More than 70% of imported wine sold in China are fake. We solve the problem by integrating a smart sensor label, called NFC (Near Field Communication) tag, with the wine bottle when the wine is produced. Consumers can check authenticity of the wine in our WineMate app when reading the tag with a smartphone.

Highly Secure Encryption

Each NFC tag has been encrypted by us through our highly secure data encryption solution, which makes clone/replicate impossible.

Real-time Tracking

Tag read events and bottle open/sealed status have been tracked real-time as well as analyzed by our fraud-prevention algorithm to identify illegal refill.

Global Unique ID

Our NFC tags are tiny, read-only chips and each of them has a global unique ID.

Simple Tapping

NFC tags transmit information across short distance to smartphones. Consumers can verify a wine by simply tapping an NFC-enabled smartphone to the tag.

About Us

For years, the widespread, outrageously-toxic fake products and food items in China have carried significant health risks to consumers and became a severe concern for everyone. We are tackling the fake issues in China by creating a simple, fun, trusted, and socially-interactive platform enabling direct communication between brands and consumers.

We combine our highly secure NFC authentication solution with cloud traceability, mobile social apps, and sophisticated recommendation engines to help consumers find trusted products that fit for their needs. Based in Silicon Valley, our team brings the most innovative and best-of-class technology to pursue our mission of driving the world forward by giving everyone faster and easier access to high-quality information.
